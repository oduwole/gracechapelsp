Reviewers

This the fun part of SL Kreativez and it's essentially one of our most priced field. Opportunity is open for literature review and we're focusing on Nigeria at the moment
Nollywood movie reviews and critique. For the greater path, reviews will be for cinematic movies and at other times, our sledgehammer will fall on our alaba-idumota made movies.
That's fun, we know but it's also a professional endeavor that requires constructive criticism that aligns to the tenets of a well-made play. We're not here for fault finding but to promote art and energize creativity

Requirements
  • Candidate for this opportunity should be a graduate or an undergraduate of any art field broadly. More specifically, someone from creative arts, theatre arts, film arts or media will be better suited.
  • There will be a brush up training for the qualified candidate and a continuous support system till they're able to master the task.
  • Passion is key. Candidate must be a big fan of motion pictures.
  • Creative and aesthetic judgment skills.
  • A creative-analytical orientation and a critical mindset is equally important for this job.
  • An unabashed spirit for the criticism that will come with the terrain. A critic must also welcome criticism cheerfully.
  • Good communications skills in writing and voice.
  • Dexterity of language and creative arts terminology. Candidate in addition to an excellent prowess of the English language, must be able to speak any one or two of these languages fluently: Yoruba, Igbo, Hausa, Efik and Benin.
What we Expect from you?
  • Attend theatrical productions, movie premiere and cinema.
  • Review and critique theatrical productions and movies.
  • Read, review and critique literatures.
