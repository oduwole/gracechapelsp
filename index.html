Who We Are

Grace Chapel represents a parish within The Redeemed Christian Church of God. The parish is deeply devoted to fostering a vibrant church environment where members receive nourishment through unwavering teachings, fervent prayer, and profound worship experiences.

Central to our ethos is the emphasis on community, endeavouring to cultivate a supportive and compassionate environment. Grace Chapel serves as a welcoming home where all individuals are embraced with the boundless love of God, ensuring that everyone finds a sense of belonging and warmth in our midst.
